Asking simple questions is very useful.

"How are you?": {{< french "Comment allez-vous" >}}? (formal) or {{< french "Comment vas-tu" >}}? (informal).
"What is your name?": {{< french "Comment vous appelez-vous" >}}? (formal) or {{< french "Comment t'appelles-tu" >}}? (informal).
"Where are you from?": {{< french "D'où venez-vous" >}}? (formal) or {{< french "D'où viens-tu" >}}? (informal).
