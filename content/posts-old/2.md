Let's learn some more common phrases.

To say "Good morning" or "Good day": {{< french "Bonjour" >}}.
To say "Good evening": {{< french "Bonsoir" >}}.
To say "Good night": {{< french "Bonne nuit" >}}.

For farewells:
"Goodbye": {{< french "Au revoir" >}}.
"See you soon": {{< french "À bientôt" >}}.
"See you tomorrow": {{< french "À demain" >}}.
